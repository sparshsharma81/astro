**Shad Bala Summary**

- **Sthana Bala** (Positional Strength): Sign + house + planetary position
- **Kaala Bala** (Temporal Strength): Day/night + season + tithi + hora
- **Dig Bala** (Directional Strength): Optimal house for each planet
  - Sun optimal in 10th, Moon in 4th, Mars in 10th
  - Mercury in 1st, Jupiter in 1st, Venus in 4th, Saturn in 7th
- **Cheshta Bala** (Motional Strength): Speed + retrograde status
- **Drig Bala** (Aspectual Strength): Beneficial aspects received
- **Naisargika Bala** (Natural Strength): Inherent planet power
  - Sun 60, Moon 51, Mars 51, Mercury 45, Jupiter 45, Venus 42, Saturn 50

## KEY CALCULATION CONCEPTS

### Atmakaraka (Soul Significator)
- **Definition:** Planet with highest degree (excluding nodes)
- **Calculation:** Find planet with max degrees + minutes + seconds÷60
- **Importance:** Represents soul's primary karmic lesson
- **Interpretation:** Most important planet for understanding life direction

### Shadbala Strength Scale
- **Range:** 0 to 100+ (varies by component)
- **Interpretation:** 
  - 50-100: Good strength
  - 30-50: Medium strength
  - Below 30: Weak planet
- **Usage:** Determine planet effectiveness

### Vimsottari Dasa Cycle
- **Total Length:** 120 years
- **Calculation:** Based on birth Moon's nakshatra position
- **Usage:** Predict major life events and timing
- **Accuracy:** Most reliable dasa system in Vedic astrology

### Dignity Levels
- **Exalted:** Planet at peak strength (excellent results)
- **Own Sign:** Planet in ruled sign (good performance)
- **Moolatrikona:** Planet's most powerful range (strongest)
- **Debilitated:** Planet at lowest strength (challenging results)
- **Neutral Sign:** Medium performance
